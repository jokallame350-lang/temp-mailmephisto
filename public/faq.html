FAQ

Common questions about Mephisto Temp Mail.

What is a temp mail / disposable email?

A disposable email is a temporary inbox you can use instead of your personal email to protect privacy and reduce spam.

How do I use Mephisto Temp Mail?

Open the website, copy your disposable email address, and use it wherever an email is required. Incoming messages appear in the inbox.

How long does the inbox stay active?

Disposable inboxes are temporary. Availability may change and messages/inboxes can be removed automatically.

Can I send emails from the temp address?

The Service is primarily for receiving emails. Sending may not be supported (depending on current features).

Why didn’t I receive an email?

Some websites block disposable email domains, and some emails can be delayed or filtered. Try refreshing the inbox or generating a new address.

Is it safe?

Temp mail helps reduce spam and protects your personal inbox. However, do not use it for sensitive accounts (banking, critical services) because inboxes are temporary.

Can I recover deleted messages?

No. Once removed, messages typically cannot be recovered.

Do you store personal data?

We aim to minimize data collection. Refer to the Privacy Policy for details about how data is handled.

Can I use this for verification codes?

Yes, many users do. But some services block temp mail domains or require a permanent email.

How can I report abuse?

If you see abuse related to the Service, contact us via the contact method provided on the website and include relevant details.
